Masomo WiFi

Masomo WiFi is an intuitive platform that empowers WiFi sellers to efficiently manage and sell access. Users can discover your WiFi network on their devices, and upon selection, they are seamlessly redirected to complete payment via M-Pesa and connect to their chosen package. Each seller can integrate multiple routers, maximizing coverage and accessibility for their customers.
